Differences Between HTML Tags: section and article

The <section> Tag

The <section> tag is used to define a section within a webpage, often with a heading. It's like a chapter or a distinct content block. In this example, we'll create a website layout:

Website Layout: Main Sections

Section: About Us

We are a company committed to providing high-quality products to our customers.

Section: Services

Our services include web development, design, and digital marketing.

The <article> Tag

The <article> tag represents a self-contained piece of content that can stand alone. It's like a single news article or a blog post. Here's a real-life example:

Blog: Latest Articles

Article: Top Web Design Trends in 2023

Explore the latest web design trends that are shaping the digital landscape in 2023.

Article: The Impact of AI in E-Commerce

Discover how artificial intelligence is transforming the e-commerce industry.

Summary

The <section> tag is used to group related content, making it suitable for website sections. The <article> tag is used for self-contained content pieces, such as individual blog posts or news articles. This helps organize and structure the webpage effectively.
