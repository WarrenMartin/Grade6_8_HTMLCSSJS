Understanding HTML Tags: section, article, and footer

The <section> Tag

The <section> tag is a semantic container that groups related content. It's often used to divide a page into different sections. Here's an example:

Real life example of section

Appetizers

Start your meal with our delicious appetizers.

Main Courses

Explore our selection of main courses.

Desserts

Satisfy your sweet tooth with our desserts.

The <article> Tag

The <article> tag represents a self-contained piece of content that could be distributed and reused independently. It's often used for blog posts, news articles, and similar content. Here's an example:

<article>
 <h2>Article Title</h2>
 <p>This is the content of the article.</p>
</article>

Real life Examples of Articles

Exploring the Beautiful Beaches of Bali

Join me on a journey to the stunning beaches of Bali, where white sands and crystal-clear waters await...

The <footer> Tag

The <footer> tag is used to define a footer for a section, article, or the entire page. It typically contains information about the author, copyright, and links to related documents. Here's an example:
