Bootstrap Badges

Badges in Bootstrap are used to highlight or label information. They can be applied to various elements like buttons to indicate status, notifications, or other contextual information. 

            <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary">
                Notifications <span class="badge badge-light">5</span>
            </button>

Different contextual badges can be used to represent different states:

  • Primary
  • Secondary
  • Success
  • Danger
  • Warning
  • Info
  • Light
  • Dark
