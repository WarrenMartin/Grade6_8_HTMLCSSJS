Active and Disabled Button

Active Button

            
<button>Click Me</button>

The button above demonstrates the :active pseudo-class. When you click and hold it, the background color changes to a darker shade (#999) to indicate the active state.

Disabled Button

            
<button disabled>Disabled</button>

The button above demonstrates the :disabled pseudo-class. The cursor changes to "not-allowed," indicating that it cannot be clicked.
