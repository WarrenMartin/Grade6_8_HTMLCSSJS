Introduction to Scrollspy

Scrollspy is a Bootstrap feature that updates the navigation bar based on scroll position. It enhances the user experience by highlighting the corresponding navigation link as the user scrolls through different sections of a webpage.

Step 1: Include Scrollspy Attributes

Enable Scrollspy behavior by adding data attributes to your body tag.

<body data-spy="scroll" data-target="#myNavbar" data-offset="50">

Step 2: Update Navigation Links

Ensure your navigation links correspond to the sections with proper IDs.

<a class="nav-link" href="#section1">Introduction</a>

Step 3: Activate Scrollspy with JavaScript

Include a script to activate Scrollspy. This initializes Scrollspy on your page.

<script> $('body').scrollspy({ target: '#myNavbar' }); </script>
