Cards in Bootstrap

Cards are flexible and extensible content containers in Bootstrap. They allow you to display various types of content, such as images, text, links, and more, in a consistent and visually appealing manner.

Cards are widely used in web design to organize and present information in a modular and visually pleasing way. They are versatile and can be customized using Bootstrap classes to match the overall design of your website.

Classes for Simple Cards

To create a simple card in Bootstrap, you can use the following classes:

  • card: The main container for the card.
  • card-body: The content area of the card.
  • card-title: The title of the card.
  • card-text: The main text content of the card.

Example: Adding a Card to Your Webpage

            <div class="card" style="width: 18rem;">
                <img src="https://via.placeholder.com/150" class="card-img-top" alt="Sample Image">
                <div class="card-body">
                    <h5 class="card-title">Sample Card Title</h5>
                    <p class="card-text">This is a sample card with some placeholder text. You can customize the content based on your needs.</p>
                </div>
            </div>
