Typography

Typography refers to the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. It involves selecting fonts, font sizes, line spacing, and other text properties to enhance the visual communication of your content.

Examples of Different Fonts in a Table

Font Family Sample Text
Arial, sans-serif The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Times New Roman, serif The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Code Snippet

<table>
    <tr>
        <th>Font Family</th>
        <th>Sample Text</th>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Arial, sans-serif</td>
        <td style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>Times New Roman, serif</td>
        <td style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', serif;">The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.</td>
    </tr>
</table>

Output

Font Family Sample Text
Arial, sans-serif The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Times New Roman, serif The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
