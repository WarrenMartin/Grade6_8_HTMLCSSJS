Understanding the Box Model

The CSS Box Model is a fundamental concept in web development. It defines how elements are structured within a webpage.

Padding

Padding is the space inside the border of an element. It separates the content from the border.

Padding is the space between the content and the border.

Padding: 20px all around

Margin

Margin is the space outside the border of an element. It separates elements from one another.

Margin is the space outside the border.

Margin: 20px all around

Border

The border is the line that surrounds the padding and content of an element.

Border is the line surrounding the padding and content.

Border: 5px solid red
